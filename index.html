Welcome to The Animal World

There are many kinds of animals in the world. Some animals are big.Some animals are small. We can see big animals like bears,zebras,lions,tigers and rhinos in the zoo.

All these animals have four legs. Do you know that the whale is the largest living animal? Some animals are small. Most birds, insects and worms are small animals. Four-legged animals like the mouse are small.

Birds

Most birds can fly. Some birds cannot fly. The ostrich and the emu cannot fly. They can walk and run.

Birds have two wings. Wings help them to fly.

Birds have two legs. Legs help them to walk.

Some birds like ducks and swans can swim.

Insects

Insects are small animals. They have six legs.

Some insects have wings. Wings help them to fly.

Where Do Animals Live?

Some animals live on land. The cow, the horse and the elephant are some animals which live on land

Many birds, insects and animals live on trees.

Some animals live in water. Some animals live near water.
